Stephanie Contreras

Hello, my name is Stephanie Contreras, and I am an undergraduate student at Boise State University pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology. My portfolio is a reflection of my journey through various artistic media from building interactive environments in Unreal Engine and designing intuitive UX/UI experiences to exploring the technical side of web development and animation. While I am a multidisciplinary artist at heart, I am currently focusing my growth toward becoming a 3D Modeler, blending these different skill sets to create immersive digital art.

3D Environment Design

Developed a foundational proficiency in Unreal Engine, specifically focusing on Material application and 3D asset integration. Gained experience in procedural workflows for generating foliage and environmental assets, as well as utilizing Landscape tools to create immersive terrains.

Digital Illustration

In this Digital Illustration project, I explored the intersection of reality and fiction by integrating original photography with digital painting. I utilized a reference photo taken on campus as a foundation for the environment, then layered stylized fictional elements over the scene. This process allowed me to advance my proficiency in Photoshop, specifically focusing on compositing, perspective drawing, and color matching.
